Ways to Volunteer

LBEF Health Club has designed several volunteering models to enable individuals get involved in community despite their time constraints. Volunteering is made easy as it caters to the individual’s interests and convenienc, time availability and location.

  • Weekend Volunteering: Spare a few hours on weekends. This is ideal for professionals and college students.
  • Weekday Volunteering: Give few hours for 2-3 days during the week. This is suitable for housewives and retirees.
  • LBEF Vacation: Volunteer every day for 1 month or more. Typically, college students serve during their semester breaks.
